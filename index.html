FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does PRYSM connect to my Amazon account?+
PRYSM connects via Amazon official Selling Partner API (SP-API). You authorize the connection through Amazon secure OAuth flow. No passwords are shared and full data ownership stays with you at all times.
How fresh is the data?+
Most data refreshes every 2-4 hours. Sales and order data can be near real-time depending on your plan. Inventory data syncs every 6 hours.
Can I manage multiple Amazon accounts?+
Absolutely. PRYSM supports multiple accounts and marketplaces in a single dashboard. You can switch between accounts or view aggregate data across all of them.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to get started.
Can I export data?+
Yes, every table in PRYSM has a three-dot export menu with XLSX, CSV, and Print options. You can export any view of your data at any time.
